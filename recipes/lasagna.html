Lasagna Recipe.

Description.

Lasagna is a baked italian dish made with layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, a rich sauce (often with tomatoes and ground meat), creamy bechamel sauce, and plenty of cheese (typically mozzarella and permesan). The layers are assembled and then baked until bubbly and golden brown, creating a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingridents

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beeg
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Brown the Meat: Cook ground beef and/or Italian sausage in a pan until browned. Drain off any extra fat. Add chopped onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  2. Make the Sauce: Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes to the meat mixture. Add your favorite herbs and spices. Let it simmer.
  3. Mix the Cheese: In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, some Parmesan cheese, and a little salt and pepper.
  4. Layer it Up: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Then, layer with lasagna noodles, the ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers until you've used all the ingredients, finishing with a layer of sauce and mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake in the oven until bubbly and the noodles are tender. Remove the foil for the last bit of baking to let the cheese get nice and melted and slightly browned.
  6. Rest: Let the lasagna sit for a few minutes after taking it out of the oven before cutting and serving. This helps it hold its shape.

    7. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
